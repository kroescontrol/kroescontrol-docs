# Get to Know Kroescontrol

Learn more about our history, vision and what Kroescontrol stands for.

## Key points

- Kroescontrol is an IT consultancy and skilled team founded in 2014 by Serge Kroes, focused on infrastructure automation and Cloud Native technologies
- We are a team of experienced IT professionals operating at a high level in Platform teams, with expertise in Kubernetes, Cloud Native technologies, Observability and SRE Engineering
- Our approach combines personal development, knowledge sharing and an informal, professional work culture

## Detailed information

### The Kroescontrol story

Kroescontrol emerged from founder Serge Kroes' passion for IT, entrepreneurship and knowledge sharing. After a career in hospitality, Serge made the switch to IT as a consultant in 2008. He specialized in Linux and cloud engineering and worked on various projects at companies like eBay, FedEx, NXP, Rabobank and KPN.

Over the years, Serge discovered the value of knowledge sharing and motivating others. This ultimately led to founding Kroescontrol in 2014, with the goal of bringing together people who motivate each other, stimulate development and have fun together. The common thread: collaboration, knowledge sharing and helping each other grow.

### Our specialization

At Kroescontrol, we specialize in infrastructure automation, with Cloud Native engineering at its core. Our team consists of experts in:

- Platform Engineering
- Cloud Native Transformation
- Observability & Monitoring
- SRE Engineering
- DevOps Consultancy

We work with modern open-source tools and have deep knowledge of cloud environments like Azure, AWS and GCP. By applying extensive automation, we deliver complete application deployments where all infrastructure is implemented as code.

### Our work environment

At Kroescontrol, people come first. We believe diversity is the foundation for innovation, job satisfaction and growth. That's why we look for "ambassadors": enthusiastic and proactive people who not only apply their technical skills but are also open to collaboration and knowledge sharing.

The atmosphere is relaxed and informal. We often share common technical interests and are open to new ideas. This creates space to experiment and innovate. With a small, driven organization, we keep communication lines short and value initiative and personal input.

### Our future vision

We want to continue growing with multiple engineers and build a solid foundation through our skilled team and consultancy. Additionally, we strive for partnerships with clients to develop innovation projects together.

For us, it's essential that assignments match our engineers' ambitions. There must be a mutual connection, so you can reach your full potential as a professional while the client achieves their goals. Your development is central: when you operate at your best, it benefits both your job satisfaction and the quality of our services.

## Frequently asked questions

### What is Kroescontrol's focus?
Our focus is on infrastructure automation in the broadest sense, with specializations in Kubernetes, Cloud Native technologies, Observability and SRE Engineering.

### What's the work environment like at Kroescontrol?
The work environment is people-focused, informal and aimed at innovation. Everyone gets the chance to develop in an open and stimulating environment with short communication lines.

### What makes Kroescontrol different from other IT companies?
Our combination of permanent employment with flexibility, personal approach, focus on knowledge sharing and our extensive benefits like the Engineer Budget and Mobility Budget distinguish us from other IT companies.
